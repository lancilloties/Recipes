Oatmeal Cookies

Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups (375 ml) unbleached all-purpose flour
1 1/2 cups (375 ml) quick-cooking rolled oats
1/2 teaspoon (2.5 ml) baking soda
1/4 teaspoon (1 ml) salt
1/2 cup (125 ml) unsalted butter, softened
1 1/4 cups (310 ml) brown sugar
1/4 cup (60 ml) canola oil
1 teaspoon (5 ml) vanilla extract
1 egg
1/2 or 1 cup of chocolate/cranberries/raisins

Instructions:
1 - With the rack in the middle position, preheat the oven to 190 c (375 f). Line 2 large baking sheets with parchment paper.
2 - In a bowl, combine flour, oats, baking soda and salt.
3 - In another bowl, cream the butter, brown sugar, oil and vanilla with an electric mixer.
4 - Add the egg and beat until smooth. At low speed or with a wooden spoon, combine with the dry ingredients.
5 - With a 60 ml(1/4 cup) ice cream scoop, place 6 to 7 dough balls on each baking sheet. Press the balls to about 2 cm thick.
6 - Bake one sheet at a time about 12 min or until lightly browned.

Notes:
Let cool on a wire rack.
Makes 12 cookies.
