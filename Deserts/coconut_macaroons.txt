Coconut Macaroons

Ingredients:
2/3 cup flour
5 1/2 cups flaked coconut
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 can (14 ounces) sweetened condensed milk
2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Instructions:
1 - Preheat oven to 350 F(175 C). Line cookie sheets with parchment paper or aluminum foil.
2 - In a large bowl stir in the flour, coconut and salt. Stir in the sweetened condensed milk and vanilla using your hands until well blended.
3 - Use an ice cream scoop to drop dough onto the prepared cookie sheets. Cookies should be golf ball size.
4 - Bake for 12 to 15 min in the oven until coconut is toasted.
