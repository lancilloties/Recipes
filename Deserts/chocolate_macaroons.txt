Chocolate Macaroons

Ingredients:
1/2 cup (125 ml) of butter
1/2 cup (125 ml) of milk
1 1/2 cup (375 ml) of sugar
6 tablespoons cocoa
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
3 cups (750 ml) large oats
1 cup (250 ml) finely chopped coconut

Instructions:
1 - Combine the butter, milk, sugar & cocoa in a small cauldron to heat at mid strength.
2 - Bring to boil, reduce heat & let boil for 4-5 min whilst mixing.
3 - Remove from fire and add vanilla.
4 - Add oats & coconut mixed into the cauldron. Wait for the oats to be very humid.
5 - Use a spoon to spread the macarons on a parchment paper.
6 - Wait for them to harden & dry.

Notes:
Must be kept in a sealed container.
Can be kept frozen for 3 months.
