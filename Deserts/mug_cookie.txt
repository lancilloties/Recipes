Mug Cookie

Ingredients:
1 tbsp butter/margarine
1 tbsp white sugar
1 tbsp brown sugar
1/2 tsp vanilla
3 tbsp flour
1 tsp chocolate chips

Instructions:
1 - Melt butter.
2 - Add the sugars & vanilla then mix.
3 - Add the flour & mix.
4 - Add chocolate chips & mix.
5 - Cook on high in a microwave for 40-50 seconds.

Notes:
Let cool for a few minutes before eating.
Don't leave your metal spoon in it, it gets very hot and burns your tongue.
You can swap the chocolate chips for cocoa & it's still good.
