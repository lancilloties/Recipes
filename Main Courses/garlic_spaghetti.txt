Garlic Spaghetti

Ingredients:
Olive oil
1 tbsp butter/margarine
Italian seasoning
Cajun spices
Salt
Parmejano
1 tbsp Garlic
Spaghetti

Instructions:
1 - Cook pasta and drain it in a strainer.
2 - Turn off the fire and reuse hot pot.
3 - Put everything in except the pasta as well as the parmejano and then mix.
4 - Once properly cooked, remove from the fire and add parmejano as well as the pasta.
5 - Mix and serve.
