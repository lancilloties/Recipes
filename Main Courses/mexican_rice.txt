Mexican Rice

Ingredients:
Oil
1 onion
1 green pepper
1 zucchini
1/2 cup corn
1 can of black beans
1 cup raw rice
3 tbsp taco spices
2 cups vegetable broth

Instructions:
1 - Cook onions and green peppers about 7 minutes.
2 - Add zucchini, corn, beans, rice, spices & broth.
3 - Cover and simmer for 20 minutes.
4 - Remove from fire and let cool for 5 minutes before serving.
