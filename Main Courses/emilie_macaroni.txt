Macaroni & cheese like emilie does

Ingredients:
2-3 cups milk
1 1/2 tbsp butter
1 tbsp flour
2-3 cups cheese
Macaroni pasta

Instructions:
1 - Cook the pasta and put in another bowl.
2 - Melt the butter and add the flour.
3 - Mix well until it's golden.
4 - Add the milk slowly and mix well.
5 - Add the cheese and melt it.
6 - Reduce the sauce by boiling.
7 - Add pasta and mix before serving.
