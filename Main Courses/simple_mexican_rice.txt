Mexican Rice

Ingredients:
1 tablespoon sriracha
1 regular can of black beans
1 cup uncooked long-grain rice
2 cups chicken broth
3/4 cup salsa
1 teaspoon garlic
lots of salt

Instructions:
1    Combine all ingredients into a medium pan. Bring to a boil, then cover mostly(open a bit) and reduce heat to 2(near low). Simmer for 25 minutes.
2    Remove from heat and let rice stand with the lid on for 10 minutes. Remove lid and fluff rice with a fork. Serve!

Notes:
Do not mix beyond the initial point of putting the ingredients in, otherwise it tends to burn.
