Pasta sauce like dad does

Ingredients:
1 Onion in small pieces
1 Beef package
2 Carrots in small pieces
3 Celeries in small pieces
Persil chopped
Basilic chopped
2 Big tomato cans
1 Big tomato sauce can
1 Small tomato paste can

Instructions:
1 - Cook onions.
2 - Add & cook steak in the same pan.
3 - Add carrots & celeries
4 - Add percil & basilic.
5 - Add all cans.
6 - Bring to a boil and let simmer for an hour.
7 - Shred all pieces.
