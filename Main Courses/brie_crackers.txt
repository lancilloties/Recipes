Brie Crackers

Ingredients:
1 Brie round cheese block
1 Apple
Cranberries
Brown sugar
Crackers

Instructions:
1 - Preheat oven to 350 F.
2 - Place brie in a large pan.
3 - Cover brie with small apple slices.
4 - Add a pinch of cranberries on apple slices.
5 - Add a few pinches of brown sugar on the brie.
6 - Place pan in oven for 15-20 minutes until brown sugar is caramelized & cheese is starting to leak.
7 - Place pan on the table & serve crackers on plates with knives for cheese service.
