Crepes

Ingredients:
2 eggs
1 cup flour
1 1/2 cup milk
2 tbsp melted butter or oil (for crepes)
1 pinch salt
2 tsp sugar
1 tsp vanilla
Butter or margarine (for pan)

Instructions:
1 - Put everything in a bowl.
2 - Mix till there's no lumps.
3 - Heat a pan with butter inside.
4 - Drop a service spoon full in a pan.
5 - Cook in a pan till brownish & flip to brown the other side.
6 - Repeat step 3 till there's no more mix left.
