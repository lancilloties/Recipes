Blueberry Sauce

Ingredients:
1/2 cup frozen blueberries
2 spoons white sugar
1 cup water

Instructions:
1 - Put everything in a hot pan starting with the blueberries, water & then the sugar.
2 - Heat pan so the water is boiling & squish the blueberries gently.
3 - Mix gently once in a while until the liquid becomes like a syrup.
3 - Serve on french toasts or other meals.
